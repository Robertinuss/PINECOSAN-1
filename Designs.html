✨⛓️Andrómeda Shun⛓️✨
Will Shun be the most powerful bronze knight? There is a lot of debate about it but there is no doubt that it has the strongest technique. Shun is a very empathetic person who hates fighting and hurting others, which is why it has cost him many times to receive great beatings, but not for lack of power but for remaining stoic in the face of his principles.
Throughout the manga and anime we can see that Shun was maturing in his fighting style at first using his Andromeda Chains in defensively or passively neutralizing his enemies, trying to get his opponents to surrender without taking too much damage, but then From the terrible battle of the 12 houses we can see how Shun no longer hesitates to use his Nebular Storm if necessary to defeat his enemies. Without a doubt, he is a Saint with a big heart, but this quality is not a weakness, but his greatest strength!

🌁 City hunter 1985 🌁
City Hunter brings us closer to the Japan of the '80s with criminals, yakuza, rapists, murderers and bad people in general. Among all these underworld people, we have Ryo Saeba, a private detective and great gunslinger, who chooses his clients in a somewhat special way. He is an inveterate womanizer and women are his downfall. Although deep down he is a good-natured person, he will defend his clients with all his abilities and skills, which are not few. His friend and associate, Hideyuki Makimura, helps win over clients in this somewhat shady business. Due to occupational hazards and fate, his friend is killed and Makimura's sister, Kaori, ends up staying to help Ryo Saeba in his mission, meanwhile, the love story between Ryo and Kaori unfolds. Together they will form an attractive, fun and charismatic duo of protagonists whom we will accompany on their adventures throughout the series.
